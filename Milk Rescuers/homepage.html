Why is Milk Wasted?

Milk is often wasted in various places due to factors such as overproduction, expiration, and transportation issues. This project aims to reduce milk wastage by facilitating the donation of surplus milk and providing recycled milk to those in need.

How You Can Help:

You can contribute to this cause by either donating surplus milk or availing recycled milk. Click the respective buttons below to get started!
